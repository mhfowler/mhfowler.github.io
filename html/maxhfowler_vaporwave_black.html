a website that strips Google Maps of all natural imagery to reveal concrete poems about the structure of contemporary space
truespeak
a thought experiment and functional app that allows you to upload conversations from your text message history to a publicly viewable page on the internet
howdoispeak
a website which shows you insights about the way you text by analyzing the frequency of your word usage in your text messages over time
the capitalist t-shirt
an e-commerce store which sells t-shirts, framed prints and booty shorts
brocascoconut.com
the landing page for a pseudo-anonymous art collective mailing list curated by me and some of my friends
